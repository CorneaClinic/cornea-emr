# Pilot Expansion Criteria

**Cornea Clinic EMR — post-pilot expansion decision**

---

## Purpose

Define when the pilot can expand to unrestricted tertiary cornea clinic use.

---

## Expansion prerequisites

All items required before expansion:

- [ ] 90-day pilot protocol completed or early-exit criteria met.
- [ ] `docs/PILOT_SUCCESS_METRICS.md` targets met for 4 consecutive weeks.
- [ ] No open P1 incidents.
- [ ] All High risks resolved or formally accepted by governance.
- [ ] Weekly checklist evidence archived for full pilot period.
- [ ] Project 12 final audit scheduled.

---

## Expansion decision matrix

| Outcome | Criteria | Next step |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| **Expand** | All prerequisites met; safety status Green | Proceed to Project 12 final audit |
| **Extend pilot** | Amber status or partial metric misses | 30-day extension with corrective plan |
| **Restrict scope** | Repeated RED status or unresolved P1 | Roll back to limited workflows; governance review |

---

## Required sign-off

- Clinical lead
- Operations lead
- Technical lead
- Governance lead

Decision date:

Decision: **Expand / Extend / Restrict**
